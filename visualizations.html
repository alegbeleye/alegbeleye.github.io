Global EV Sales

Data Source: IEA Global EV Outlook 2024 (simplified figures for 2023)

This bar chart displays the leading countries and regions by the number of electric vehicles sold in 2023. Hover over the bars to see the exact sales figures in millions.

Interpretation

The visualization clearly indicates China's overwhelming dominance in the global electric vehicle market. This highlights China's central role as both a consumer and producer in the worldwide transition to electric mobility.
