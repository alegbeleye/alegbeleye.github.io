Global Genre Sales

Visualization 1: A grouped bar chart is used to effectively compare the total global sales of different genres across various platforms. The grouping by platform allows for a direct comparison of genre performance on each console.

Trends Over Time

Visualization 2: An interactive line chart is chosen to visualize sales trends over time. The interactivity allows users to focus on specific platforms and genres, making it easier to analyze and compare their performance over the years.

Regional Sales

Visualization 3: A stacked bar chart is used to show the regional sales composition for each platform. This choice effectively illustrates the regional preferences and the contribution of each region to the total sales of a platform.

Sales Performance

Visualization 4: A multi-series line chart is used to tell a story about the sales performance of the top 5 publishers over time. This visualization effectively highlights the rise and fall of these major players in the video game industry.
